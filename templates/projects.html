PROJECT PORTFOLIO

Botball

Botball is an autonomous robotics competition for high school students worldwide. Every year, a new game board is released, and teams are challenged with building and programming robots to complete tasks on that board without any human interaction or intervention. From 2013-2016, I was elected captain of Hampton Botball. During that time, I lead the team to three global top 10 finishes and a world championship victory. Check out the videos below for a taste of what exactly a botball robot looks like, and follow the github link to download the PI Motor Control and Line Following libraries I wrote for our bots!
